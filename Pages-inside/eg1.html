Description

Adyar waterfalls are around 12 kms drive from the Mangalore city. This waterfall has no official name and the locals name it after the place it is located i.e. Adyar.

Things to know !

  • Location: There are two roads which lead to this place. The route towards BC Road highway near Adyar and other from Neermarga (the road towards Volachil).
  • Famous for: Scenery
  • Things to do: Sightseeing
  • Price: No entry fee
