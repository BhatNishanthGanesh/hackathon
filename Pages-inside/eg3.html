Anantheshwara Temple Religious Site

  1. Home
  2. Anantheshwara Temple

Description

Udupi is a land of temples and one of the most frequented one is the Udupi Anantheshwara Temple. Parasurama is the main diety worshiped in this temple and he is an incarnation of Lord Vishnu. A lot of locals visit this temple and you can easily get to know about the story related to it from the locals.

Things to Know !

  • Location: Udupi, Karnataka
  • Famous for: Religious site
  • Things to do: Prayer
  • Price: No entry fee
